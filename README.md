# ☕ Coffee Shop Sales Analysis Dashboard – Power BI Project

## 📌 Project Overview
This project showcases an interactive Power BI dashboard developed for analyzing coffee shop sales performance. The report provides meaningful business insights related to sales trends, product performance, customer purchasing behavior, and store operations.

The dashboard is designed to help business owners and analysts make data-driven decisions by visualizing key performance indicators in a simple and interactive way.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze overall coffee shop sales performance
- Track daily and monthly sales trends 
- Identify top-selling products and categories
- Understand customer purchasing patterns
- Compare Weekday and Weakend sales
- Evaluate store location performance 
---

## 📊 Dashboard Details

 📌 This interactive dashboard includes the following insights:

- 📅 Yearly Sales Analysis
- 📈 Monthly Sales Trends
- 🗓️ Quarterly Sales Performance
- ☕ Product Category wise Sales
- 🔝 Top 10 Products by Sales
- 🏪 Sales by Store Location
- 📅 Daily Sales Insights
- 📊 Weekday vs Weekend Sales Comparison

The dashboard also contains interactive slicers and filters for better data exploration and analysis.

# 📊 Key Performance Metrics
Sales Records for the First Half of 2023:--

-💰 Total Sales Revenue: ₹698,812

-🧾 Total Transactions: 149,116  

-📅 Total Orders: 14,991 Units 

-📦 Total Quantity Sold: 21,440 Units

-🏪 Top Performing Store: Hell’s Kitchen

-📅 Highest Sales Month: June

-📈 Highest sales performance by day : Monday


- Sales performance was significantly **higher on weekdays** compared to weekends, showing stronger business activity during working days.

- The **Hell’s Kitchen** location achieved the highest sales among all store locations, making it the top-performing branch.

- The top-performing product categories were **Coffee and Tea**, contributing the highest share of total sales.

- The highest-selling product types were **Barista Espresso and Brewed Chai Tea**, indicating strong customer preference for premium beverage offerings.

- **Quarter II (Q2) recorded the highest sales** revenue and quantity sold compared to other quarters.

- **January** recorded the **lowest sales** performance during the reporting period.

- **Saturday** generated the lowest sales among **all weekdays**.

- The least-performing product category was **Packaged Chocolate** , showing minimal customer demand.

- The **Lower Manhattan** region recorded the lowest sales among all store locations.
